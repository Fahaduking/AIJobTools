When to use ChatGPT

✓ Use ChatGPT when you need...

Image generation from text (DALL·E), real-time web browsing and research, a massive plugin ecosystem, coding help across many languages, or the most widely-supported AI for third-party integrations. ChatGPT is also the better choice for a versatile "do anything" assistant with the largest community of tips and tutorials.

When to use Claude

✓ Use Claude when you need...

The most natural, human-sounding writing output, working with very long documents (entire books, codebases, research papers), following complex multi-step instructions precisely, or analysing uploaded PDFs on the free tier. Claude is the better choice for writers, researchers, academics, and anyone who prioritises writing quality over a broad feature set.

The honest answer: use both

Most power users have both open. Use ChatGPT for brainstorming, image generation, quick research, and coding. Use Claude for serious writing projects, analysing documents, and tasks where language quality really matters. Both have free tiers strong enough for daily use.
