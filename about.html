What we cover

Right now we cover AI tools for freelancers, students, teachers, marketers, developers, and content creators. We're expanding to designers, writers, lawyers, healthcare professionals, and more throughout 2026.

Each profession guide includes: tool-by-tool reviews with pros and cons, honest pricing information, who each tool is actually for, and a recommended starter stack so you don't have to figure it out yourself.

Our promise

We don't accept payment for tool placement. We don't write fake positive reviews. When a tool has problems, we say so. When a free tool beats a paid one, we tell you. Some links on this site may be affiliate links — but that never influences our rankings or recommendations.

Our only goal is that you leave this site knowing exactly which AI tools to use for your job — and which ones to skip.
